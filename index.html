About

Hi, I'm Akhil, and I grad student from the University of Texas at Dallas. My interests are in UI/UX Engineering & Full Stack Development. I love to create beautiful and performant products with delightful user experiences.

I've been a part of this industry for over four years and within those years, I've also had the opportunity to serve as lead software engineer for different projects.

For me, any project I get the chance to work on is a fresh chance to learn something new, interact with others who have opinions different from mine, and learn things about myself that I had no idea existed beneath the surface of what I am.

Inquisitive, energetic, goal-oriented, determined, and focused with a keen eye for details.

Well-organised person, problem solver, independent employee with high attention to detail.

I enjoy watching anime. I love playing PC games (#DOTA2forever). At my leisure, I play badminton & cricket. Fan of DC & Marvel.

I'm open for full-time positions and contract work anywhere in the world. As long as you don't mind watching me work from screens away.
